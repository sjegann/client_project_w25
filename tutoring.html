Welcome to UMSI Tutoring Services

UMSI Tutoring Program

The UMSI Tutoring Program offers free peer tutoring to support your academic success. Services include:

  • One-on-one and group tutoring sessions
  • Flexible scheduling to fit your needs
  • Support in a variety of subjects
  • Peer-to-Peer Tutoring Program available

More Information

For more information and to request a tutor, visit the official UMSI Tutoring website.

Please email us at umsi.academicsuccess@umich.edu with any questions or concern you might have.
